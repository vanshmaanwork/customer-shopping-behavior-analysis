# 🛍 Customer Shopping Behavior Analytics

An **End-to-End Data Analytics Project** analyzing customer purchasing patterns, product performance, and revenue insights using **SQL, Python, and Power BI**.

This project demonstrates a **real-world data analytics workflow** used by professional data analysts to extract meaningful business insights from raw customer transaction data.

---

# 📊 Project Overview

Understanding customer behavior is critical for businesses to improve **marketing strategies, pricing decisions, and customer retention**.

In this project, we analyze a customer shopping dataset to uncover insights related to:

* Customer purchase behavior
* Product performance
* Revenue distribution
* Discount impact on sales
* Shipping preferences
* Customer segmentation
* Subscription trends

The analysis is performed using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to simulate a real **business intelligence workflow**.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are to:

• Analyze revenue generated by different customer groups
• Identify high-performing products based on ratings and purchases
• Evaluate the impact of discounts on purchasing behavior
• Compare spending patterns based on shipping methods
• Understand whether subscription customers spend more
• Segment customers based on purchase history
• Identify repeat buyer behavior and product demand trends

# 📈 Business Questions Answered

The analysis answers several important business questions:

### 1️⃣ Revenue by Gender

What is the total revenue generated by **male vs female customers**?

### 2️⃣ Discount Behavior

Which customers used **discounts but still spent above the average purchase amount**?

### 3️⃣ Product Ratings

Which **top 5 products** have the highest average review ratings?

### 4️⃣ Shipping Type Analysis

How do purchase amounts differ between **Standard and Express shipping**?

### 5️⃣ Subscription Insights

Do **subscribed customers spend more** than non-subscribers?

### 6️⃣ Discount Strategy

Which products have the **highest percentage of purchases with discounts applied**?

### 7️⃣ Customer Segmentation

Customers are segmented into three groups:

• New Customers
• Returning Customers
• Loyal Customers

based on their purchase history.

### 8️⃣ Product Popularity

What are the **top 3 most purchased products within each category**?

### 9️⃣ Repeat Buyer Analysis

Are customers with **more than 5 previous purchases likely to subscribe**?

### 🔟 Age Group Revenue

Which **age groups contribute the most revenue**?

# 🚀 Key Insights

Some insights derived from the analysis include:

✔ Loyal customers generate the highest revenue contribution.

✔ Certain high-rated products maintain strong sales even when discounts are applied.

✔ Subscription customers tend to spend more on average.

✔ Express shipping customers often have higher purchase values.

✔ A small number of product categories contribute a large portion of total revenue.
